Project Details.

After getting wind that friend/classmate Michelle Lamont was diagnosed with breast cancer (at age 25), Christina Chern and I moved quickly to show our support. We formed a 10k team that vowed to raise $5,000 for the VCU Massey Cancer Center. I took on the task of leading a creative team to execute a fundraising campaign that would both encourage and entertain our friend. Turns out we blew through our goal and raised over $16k, produced a video series, earned the title of the Massey Cancer Center's board "favorite team of the year," and my asthmatic ass ran a 10k for the first time. Hell yes.
