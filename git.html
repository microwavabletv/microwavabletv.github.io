Project Details.

People suffer an annual tragedy of the Failed New Year's Resolution. We sought to remedy that through an app that provides structure and accountability through gamifying the experience of attaining a goal. Born from the 360iU summer intern class with the direction of dean of 360iU, Mark Avnet.
