Project Details.

Seeing a chance to be apart of the Zombie culture in Richmond, a group of my undergrad VCU Creative Advertising chums and I (1818 Productions) endeavored to put on the first ever RVA Zombie Film Festival. We were very successful and well-supported as we formed a relationship with the Byrd Theatre Foundation and Operations. When I entered the VCU Brandcenter Creative Technology track, the 2013 CT class produced a Zombiefied version of the Byrd's beloved "Litter in the Aisles" trailer. 1818 Productions managed to raise over $6,000 from this campaign and a fundraiser that also ran to educate about the Byrd's financial needs.
