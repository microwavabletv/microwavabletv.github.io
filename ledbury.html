Project Details.

Ledbury is a local shirt company based in Richmond, Virginia. They pulled me on to help with their branding to create videos to feature on their website as origin stories as well as populating content for their digital voice. I helped start "The Sharp Series," which was an initiative to engage their Facebook community as well as a teaching gig in which I prepared a 5-week course to assist in grooming and educating their in-house video production.
